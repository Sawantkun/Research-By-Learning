About

Techno Cultural Club is organizing a 2 months long team based event, Research By Learning Hackathon for the GBU students from 17th Feb to 17th April, 2022 in School of Information and Communication Technology (SOICT). A perfect opportunity for students to showcase their Excellent Writing skills and Innovation.

Perks

  • Achievement Certificates & Cash Prizes for students Top Positions in Hackathon.
  • Excellence Certificates and Consolation Prizes for Top position in Hackathon other than winners.
  • Completion Certificates for all the attendees who complete this Hackathon and submit their work .
  • Virtual Badges of different colors will be given to the good Performers on a weekly / task basis.
  • Ranking will be given to all on a task basis .
